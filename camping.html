Camping Gear

When going camping, it's important to pack the right gear. Here are some essentials:

  • Tent
  • Sleeping bag
  • Camp stove
  • Cooler
  • First aid kit

For a more complete list, check out REI's camping checklist.

Campsite Selection

Choosing the right campsite can make a big difference in your camping experience. Look for a site that:

  • Is level and free of rocks and debris
  • Has access to water
  • Is away from any potential hazards like dead trees or steep cliffs

For more tips on campsite selection, visit NPS's guide to campsite selection.

Campfire Cooking

Cooking over a campfire can be a fun and delicious way to enjoy your camping trip. Here are some tips:

  1. Bring a cast iron skillet for cooking over the fire
  2. Use foil to cook foods like potatoes and corn on the cob
  3. Bring a wire rack to cook meats over the fire

For more campfire cooking ideas, check out Taste of Home's camping recipes.
